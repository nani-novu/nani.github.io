Simple Quick Steps to seamlessly register

The process is designed to be intuitive, swift, and seamless.

1

Create Your Account

First, you need to create your account with your basic information such as names, passport photo, phone number, email address and National Identification Number (NIN).

2

Provide Educational Details

You'll be required to provide details of your qualifications and relevant academic information to verify your eligibility for employment opportunities.

3

Document Uploads

Upload your academic certificates and other relevant qualifications. These documents will help verify your credentials and eligibility for employment opportunities.

Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must be indigenes of Akwa Ibom State.

  • Provide National Identification Number (NIN) for verification of identity.

  • Applicants must be currently unemployed or seeking employment.

  • Provide relevant personal qualifications and educational information.
